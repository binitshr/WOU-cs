My Green pasta recipe

    Ingredients list
  • pasta : shells or penne : 1 servings
  • spinach : one cup chopped
  • gralic : 2 small cloves
  • seasoning : salt and pepper to taste
  • protein of choice : one serving
  • vegetables : asparagus, red bell peppers, mushrooms
  • cream cheese : 2 tbsp
  • mayonaise : 2 tbsp
  • lemon juice : 1tbsp
  • coriander : 1/2 cup chopped
    Directions
  1. Cook the pasta 2 minutes less than in the instructions of the packaging :dont forget to add salt to the water: save about 1/4th cup of pasta water
  2. take the spinach, coriander, cream cheese, mayo lime juice, garlic, and blend it in a mixer
  3. Cook the protein of your choice in a pan
  4. when the protein is almost cooked add the veggies and cook for a minute
  5. Add the sauce and cook for about 5 minutes
  6. Add the pasta water to make the sauce of your desired consistency
  7. Add the pasta and cook for 2 minutes
  8. a healthy and hearty pasta is ready to eat!
